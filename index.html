About

GODS is the official anthem for the League of Legends World Championship 2023. Each year, an anthem is created based on the themes of MOBA game League of Legends, heavily influenced by the storylines centered around the competitive professional e-sport scene.

In 2023, GODS focused haevily on players that can be colloquially referred to as Gods of the video game for their longevity in the e-sport and consistent dominance.

NewJeans

Newjeans is a K-POP group composed of five members, Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.

The members have years of exprience in the pop, dance, and fashion industry, but debuted as a group in 2022.

The name is a double entendre on "new genes" being a new generation of K-POP and the timeless style of denim.
